Culture and Heritage

Kerala is known for its classical dance forms, music, and festivals. Experience the rich cultural heritage through Kathakali performances, traditional music, and local festivals.

Kathakali Dance

Kathakali is an evolved dance form popular in the South Indian state of Kerala. This Indian classical dance involves storytelling activities through fascinating footwork and expressive gestures of face and hands accompanied by music and vocal performance.

Onam Festival

Onam is an annual harvest and cultural festival related to Hinduism that is celebrated mostly by the people of Kerala. A major annual event for Keralites, it is the official festival of the state and includes a spectrum of cultural events. Onam commemorates Vamana and the generous but egotistical daitya king Mahabali.
